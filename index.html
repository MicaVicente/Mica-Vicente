Event Details

Date: Saturday, May 18, 2024

Location: Quirino Stadium, Bantay, Ilocos Sur

Event Timings: 5:00 PM - 3:00 AM

Ticket Tiers: General Admission, VIP, Premium Seating

Facilities: Food Stalls, Parking, Restrooms

Directions: Located just outside Vigan City, accessible via Bantay Road. Public transport options include buses and jeepneys from Vigan City center.

Prohibited Items: Weapons, drugs, large bags, outside alcohol, and fireworks are prohibited.

Age Restrictions: This event is open to all ages. However, VIP and Premium Seating require 18+ for entry.

Contact Info: For questions or emergencies, contact us at vigan@concertfest.com or call +63 (77) 123-4567.
